Featured Work

Gaze Behavior & Field Dependency in VR

Accepted to ISMAR 2025, this project investigates how individual differences in field dependency influence gaze behavior and perception in virtual environments. Using the HTC Vive Pro Eye headset, I collected and analyzed eye-tracking data with Recurrence Quantification Analysis (RQA) to identify patterns that predict cybersickness susceptibility. This research sits at the intersection of cognitive psychology, data science, and immersive technology.

Spatial Cognition in VR

In this project, I study how people form and navigate spatial mental maps within virtual environments using the Meta Quest headset. I run participants through structured experiments, collect behavioral data, and troubleshoot Unity-based tasks. The goal is to understand how immersive environments affect spatial reasoning and memory compared to the real world.

Unity 3D Curriculum — Code Wiz

As a Coding & Game Development Instructor at Code Wiz, I designed and built a Unity 3D curriculum from scratch for students ages 6–16. This involved creating structured lesson plans, hands-on projects, and age-appropriate challenges that introduce students to game development, programming logic, and creative problem-solving.
